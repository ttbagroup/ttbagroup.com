We're looking for an intern to join our marketing team at TTBA

WHAT DOES IT TAKE?
Is this you?
We are on the lookout for a passionate, dynamic and creative mind to join our growing ranks. Given our entrepreneurial roots, we encourage individuality and those who take initiatives but at the end of the day, TTBA is a team effort.

For a good fit with our crew, the perfect candidate will need to love digital marketing, challenge the conventional and forget his or her comfort zone. Make mistakes but learn quickly!
Essential qualities:
— Clear understanding of the value and strength of each social media platform
— Excellent communication skills
— Excellent writing skills
— Bilingual (French, English)
Knowledge of Facebook Advertising, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator is an asset.
What we offer

This internship position is a great opportunity for students and young professionals looking for a fast-paced digital agency environment. Here's what you get:

Work on real marketing cases

Interact with clients, build and execute social media campaigns, present your results, recommend changes and evaluate objective completion.

Mentorship and personal growth

You will be joining a team of young entrepreneurs with experience in various industries. The learning curve will be steep at first but extremely rewarding later.

Paid internship

We believe that everyone should be paid for their work and we understand that even as an intern, you have to pay the bills.

Learn latest trends in marketing

Digital marketing is growing and expanding faster than ever. We make sure to stay on top of the news and implement the latest changes in our work.
Who we are
TTBA is a Montreal based marketing agency that understands how digital technology has changed the way we do business. We take our clients' idea from a sketch on a paper to a fully integrated marketing campaign. From social media to branding, web design and SEO, with innovative strategies and tools on the cutting edge, we help companies build their online authority and deliver tangible results.
TTBA Group is made up of a core team of marketing consultants, with disciplines ranging from sales, social media and web development to video production and public relations. We're ready to take on a new team member and are now seeking an intern, for the social media manager position, to join our team.
